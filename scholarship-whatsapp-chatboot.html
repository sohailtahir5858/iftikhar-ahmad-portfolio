Scholarships WhatsApp Chatbot Using ChatGPT

This project involved developing a WhatsApp chatbot to assist students in finding scholarships by leveraging the power of ChatGPT. The solution aims to provide real-time, personalized responses to users, helping them easily find relevant scholarships based on their needs and qualifications.

UpWork Client

  • Industry: Education Tech
  • Size: 100+
  • Website: clientsite.com
The client is an education technology company looking to provide automated scholarship assistance to students across different countries through a conversational WhatsApp chatbot.
  • Requirement: Create a chatbot using ChatGPT that can handle multiple users simultaneously.
  • Requirement: Provide accurate and updated scholarship information.
  • Requirement: Integrate the chatbot seamlessly with WhatsApp to offer a user-friendly interface.

Project Overview

The project aimed to build a ChatGPT-powered chatbot accessible through WhatsApp, making it easy for students to search for scholarships. The chatbot helps users find scholarships based on their eligibility, education level, location, and field of study, streamlining the scholarship search process.

The Challenge

One of the key challenges was ensuring that the chatbot could handle large volumes of users simultaneously without compromising performance. Additionally, keeping the scholarship data current and ensuring the chatbot provided personalized responses to each student was critical for the project's success.

The Approach & Solution

The chatbot was built using OpenAI's GPT-3 for natural language processing and integrated with WhatsApp's API for real-time communication. A robust database was created to store and update scholarship information. To ensure scalability, the backend was designed using FastAPI with Redis for handling multiple requests concurrently.

The chatbot was successfully deployed, offering students a seamless way to interact via WhatsApp. This resulted in significant time savings and ease for students searching for scholarships across various platforms.
