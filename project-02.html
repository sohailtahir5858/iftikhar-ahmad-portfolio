S&P 500 Mini Future Contract Forecasting

In this project, we focused on forecasting the S&P 500 Mini futures contract prices using advanced machine learning techniques. The data was sourced from the CME Group, containing detailed time series data such as ask and bid prices, alongside other key market features. We leveraged state-of-the-art transformer-based models to make accurate predictions across multiple time horizons. The model was trained on Google Cloud Platform (GCP), utilizing powerful GPUs to optimize training speed and performance.

Project Overview

This project focused on forecasting the prices of S&P 500 Mini futures contracts using advanced machine learning techniques. The time series data included essential features like ask and bid prices. We utilized the Temporal Fusion Transformer (TFT) model to make accurate forecasts across different time horizons. Training was done on Google Cloud Platform (GCP), leveraging GPU power to speed up and optimize the model.

The Challenge

Forecasting market prices is always challenging due to the rapidly changing market conditions. Additionally, integrating the forecasting model with trading platforms like NinjaTrader posed difficulties due to compatibility issues, documentation gaps, and high transaction fees, which limited its usability.

The Approach & Solution

We utilized the Temporal Fusion Transformer (TFT) model to forecast S&P 500 Mini futures prices based on historical data from CME Group. The model's accuracy was evaluated using MAE, RMSE, MAPE, and SMAPE. Although we initially attempted integration with NinjaTrader, we switched to Ironbeam due to its better documentation and lower fees, which improved our trading infrastructure.

The model was successfully integrated with Ironbeam's API, resulting in faster and more accurate forecasts for trading decision support.
